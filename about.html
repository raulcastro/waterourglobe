YOU HAVE THE POWER

Water our globe

The Cofounders of Water Our Globe, Braydon Denham and Shawn Cameron have been friends for 27 years, but they probably could have never imagined that Water Our Globe would be part of their journey.

They both took vastly different paths in life. Braydon and his family now live in Fort Saskatchewan, and he has worked in the auto industry for the majority of his adult life. Shawn is now based in Calgary, but he took his journey to places such as Mexico, Panama, and the Dominican Republic.

Although their journeys were immensely different they crossed paths and engaged in a conversation regarding impoverished countries and the truly important needs in life. Throughout this conversation one thing became evidently clear. They both understood that they are simply lucky to have been born in North America and to be supplied with everything they ever needed. The conversation started with an idea to go to Africa and build schools and help with the education programs.

While this is still a future goal for Water Our Globe, Braydon and Shawn both agreed that it would be more efficient to commence with a drilling program that supplied impoverished countries with clean water wells. Water Our Globe was essentially born that evening.

The short term goal for Water Our Globe is to drill 1-2 clean water wells in 2017. The aim is to increase that number to 3-4 in 2018. As soon as it is financially viable they will implement their next phase regarding schools and education systems.

We hope that this cause brings as much joy to you as it does to us. Water Our Globe would love to hear from you and discuss any questions that you may have about our program. You can contact us at info@WaterOurGlobe.org. We look forward to hearing from each and every one of you!
